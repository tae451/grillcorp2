When I ask people why they went with a custom PC over a prebuilt one, the most important factor is usually the degree of customizability that is available. It’s important to consider that this can be a double edged sword – an improperly configured system may lead to you spending additional hundreds of dollars unnecessarily. It can be tempting to find the flashiest models of each component, but unless you have an unlimited budget, every unnecessary spend will be detracting from the other components.



On this webpage I’ll be writing up some information I feel could be useful to a new PC builder – however this is only designed to be supplementary knowledge, so make sure to do additional research on the fundamentals!
