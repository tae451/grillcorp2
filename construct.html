Regarding Assembly...




Assembly is simpler than people usually expect, but there are still a few pitfalls you should watch out for. The proprietary connectors that come with some components (usually RGB related) aren’t always as robust as the standard connectors, and may be possible to plug them into the wrong places and short something. Cable management is optional, but make sure no cable is floating near any moving parts. Make sure the airflow directions make sense – keep in mind that warm air rises!
